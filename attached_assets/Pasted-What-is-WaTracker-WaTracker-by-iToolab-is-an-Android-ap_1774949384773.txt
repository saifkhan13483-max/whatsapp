What is WaTracker?

WaTracker (by iToolab) is an Android application designed to track WhatsApp activity — primarily the Last Seen status, online/offline notifications, chat monitoring, and "View Once" messages. It is available on Google Play and works by linking to a WhatsApp account via an authorized connection method.

How WaTracker Connects to WhatsApp:

WaTracker uses two distinct connection methods depending on what feature you want to use:

1. WhatsApp Account Linking (for Last Seen & Online Tracking)

This method connects your own WhatsApp account to the WaTracker app as a "bridge" to monitor other contacts. Here's how it works step by step:

Step 1 – Add a Contact: You launch WaTracker, tap "Add Contact," and enter the target contact's name and phone number (the person you want to track).
Step 2 – Configure Notifications: You toggle on/off online and offline notifications as desired, then press "Start Tracking."
Step 3 – Connect WhatsApp: You press the "Connect WhatsApp" button inside the app, which prompts you to link your own WhatsApp account to the app.
Step 4 – Enter WhatsApp Details: You follow on-screen instructions to authorize the connection between WaTracker and WhatsApp on your device.
Step 5 – Activate Tracking: Once the account is successfully linked, you press "Go to Activate" → "Activate," and WaTracker begins monitoring the target contact's online/offline activity in real time.
Essentially, WaTracker uses your own WhatsApp account as a logged-in session to passively observe when your tracked contacts come online or go offline, without the target person being notified.

2. QR Code Scanning (for Chat & Message Monitoring)

For the more advanced feature of reading WhatsApp chats, WaTracker uses a QR code-based method — similar to WhatsApp Web. Here's how it works:

You launch WaTracker and tap "Start."
The app generates a QR code on the screen.
You then physically pick up the phone that is already logged into the target WhatsApp account and scan the QR code using WhatsApp's "Linked Devices" feature.
Once scanned, WaTracker loads and syncs all WhatsApp chats and content from that account.
⚠️ Important Note: This QR method requires physical access to the target's phone and effectively uses WhatsApp's own "Linked Devices" functionality — the same mechanism used by WhatsApp Web/Desktop. This is technically an authorized session from WhatsApp's perspective, but using it without the account owner's consent may be illegal and a violation of privacy laws in many jurisdictions.

Key Technical Mechanism Summary:

Feature	Connection Method
Last Seen / Online Status Tracking	Link your own WhatsApp account via in-app authorization
Chat / Message Monitoring	QR Code scan on the target's phone (like WhatsApp Web)
View Once Messages	QR Code scan + account linking
